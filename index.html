The Problem

Why SA Teachers Need AI — Now

South African primary education faces systemic challenges that technology can help solve.

📊

Overwhelming Ratios

Teacher-learner ratios unchanged since apartheid — often 1:35+ in public schools, leaving learners underserved.

📝

Admin Overload

Administrative tasks consume ~60% of teacher time, leaving less for lesson prep, feedback, and pastoral care.

📖

CAPS Complexity

Meeting CAPS requirements while addressing diverse learner needs creates complex planning challenges.

💻

Limited Tech Access

Good EdTech tools exist, but most aren't accessible, contextual, or designed for under-resourced schools.
